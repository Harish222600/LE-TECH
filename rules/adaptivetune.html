Rule Book

ADAPTIVE TUNE

Basic rules about this event:

A dance competition where participants improvise movements to spontaneously played music.
Rules and Regulations:
Participation: Each team consists of 2 members.
Performance Duration: Participants
are allotted 2 to 3 minutes for their performance.
Music Selection: Music is played on the spot, requiring dancers to adapt their moves in real-time.
Judging Criteria: Performances are evaluated based on expression, attire, choreography, and energy.
Conduct: Participants must display good sportsmanship and respect towards fellow competitors. Any form of vulgarity or obscenity is strictly prohibited and may result in disqualification.
Identification: A valid college ID card is required for registration and participation. Judges' Decision: The decision of the judges is final and binding.
