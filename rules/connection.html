Rule Book

Connection

Basic rules about this event:

Rules & Regulations:
Team Composition: Participants can compete as teams of two person.
Match Format: All participants compete simultaneously.
Answer Submission: First team to ring the buzzer will have the chance to answer.
Time Limit: Each question has a 15 second time limit.
Elimination: Negative mark will be provided for every Incorrect answer.
Winning Criteria: The top 1 team with the highest scores in the final round will be declared winners.
Tiebreaker: In case of a tie, an extra question will be given to determine the winner.
Judging Panel: A panel of judges will verify all answers before confirming the winners.
Round Details:
Round 1: Preliminary Round (Knockout Stage)
Participants: 2 person per team can participate.
Format: All the team is given with different set of images and must determine the connection.
Scoring: 1 point per correct answer.
Elimination: The top 5 teams move to the next round.
Round 2: Semi-Finals
Format:
Each team receives more challenging image sets.
Scoring: 2 points per correct answer.
Elimination: The top 3 teams advance to the final round.
Round 3: Final Round
Format:
Each team receives more challenging image sets.
Scoring: 5 points per correct answer.
Winner Selection: team with the highest scores are declared winners
(only one team will be the winner)
