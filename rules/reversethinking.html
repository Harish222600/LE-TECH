Rule Book

REVERSE THINKING

Basic rules about this event:

One-Line Description:
Individual Participant answer questions irrelevantly for 2 minutes.
Brief Description:
Participants have to think opposite or reverse of the given statement or scenario. Participants answer questions irrelevantly for 2 minutes to spark lateral thinking and add fun. This game encourages out-of-the-box thinking and creativity. For example if the opponent will ask the question like ("what is your name?). The participant reply should be in a irrelevant way like("I like mango..")
This game encourages out-of-the-box thinking and creativity.
Rules & Regulations:
1. Relevant answers.
2. repetitions.
3. one-word answers.
4. remaining silent for more than 5 seconds result in disqualification.
5. players are not allowed to ask question. Team Composition: Individual participant.
Winning Criteria: The first participant successfully completed their task declared as a winner.
Time Limit: If no participant finishes within 2 minutes the participant will be disqualified.
